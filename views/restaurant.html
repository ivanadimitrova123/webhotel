Restaurants and Lobby Bar

KAMENO RESTAURANT

The only place which combines in itself coziness and style, impressive panoramic views and works of art. From the delicious home-made pancakes to Popieta made of salmon and shrimp with saffron risotto as a side dish – our master chefs will offer you traditional dishes and impressive culinary temptations from the European cuisine. The restaurant is a perfect place for company events, private parties and official receptions.

SPA Restaurant Olives

An innovative concept for the comfort and convenience of our guests. Don’t waste precious spa time for changing clothes and official luncheons. The Olives Restaurant is located next to the Spa and Wellness Zone and is in immediately proximity to the pools. A pleasant setting, relaxing music and home-made dishes, barbecue on charcoal, Italian specialties and fresh salads await you here.

LOBBY BAR

The coziness of muted light, modern classical pieces artfully performed on the grand piano, a rich selection of cigars and high-quality drinks, home-made designer-made desserts and anassortment of teas. The Lobby Bar of is the perfect place for rest, meetings, spontaneous conversations.
